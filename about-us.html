About Us

Shanks Enterprises LLC is a global real estate investment company based in California. Our goal is to create long-term investments with steady returns for risk-adverse investors. By diversifying our properties in 23 countries, we are minimizing the risk our investors take on when they join us. We both lease and manage our properties which gives us a lot of control on maximizing equity. We have an 8.2 billion USD in equity and 14.5 billion USD in debt. Our executives are risk adverse, so their goal is to use as little leverage as possible. While not highly leveraging ourselves puts a cap on our returns, it prevents us from experiencing major losses when the economy takes a downturn.
